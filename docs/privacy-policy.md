🌐 PRIVACY POLICY
FunPro Space Privacy Policy
Effective Date: June 24, 2026

Welcome to FunPro Space ("we," "our," or "us"). We provide an AI-driven underwriting platform designed for the Base blockchain ecosystem. This Privacy Policy outlines how your data is collected, processed, stored, and shared when you interact with our decentralized application (dApp).

Web3 Core Notice:
By using this application, you interact directly with the public Base Mainnet blockchain network. Blockchain transactions are public, immutable, and permanent. Please do not submit any information you wish to keep entirely confidential to on-chain functions.

1. Information We Collect and Process
We leverage industry-leading third-party infrastructure to process your identity and app interactions securely. Here is exactly what we collect:

Authentication & Identity (Privy): To onboard smoothly, Privy processes and verifies your Web3 cryptographic public wallet address, email address, and mobile phone number via secure One-Time Passwords (OTP).

Operational & Business Inputs: We collect your "Operational Strategy Canvas" inputs, including business concepts, project timelines, and requested funding milestones.

On-Chain Data: Your public wallet address, metadata, and requested USDC funding distributions are captured when interacting with our smart contracts.

2. Third-Party Data Processing
Your data is processed by the following third-party services to deliver core application functionality:

Google Generative AI / Gemini: Your business ideas and canvas inputs are transmitted to Google's Generative AI APIs (@google/genai) to power the Gemini Underwriting Engine, validating business readiness and generating expense tranches.

Privy: Manages your secure progressive onboarding data, linking contact information to web3 identities seamlessly.

Vercel AI SDK: Orchestrates the text streaming pipelines between our frontend user interface and the Google AI computational backend.

3. Data Storage and Retention

Off-Chain Profiles (MongoDB): Your verified contact profiles synced from Privy, alongside your system generated project assessments, are stored securely using MongoDB. We retain this profile data as long as your account remains active within our ecosystem.

On-Chain Records (Base Network): Budgets logged on-chain via Coinbase Smart Wallets/OnchainKit are anchored directly onto the Base Mainnet production network. This data is permanently written to a public distributed ledger and cannot be updated, modified, or deleted by any party.

4. Your Rights
Depending on your jurisdiction, you may request access to, or deletion of, your off-chain profile data stored in our MongoDB system. However, please note that we exercise no control over data committed to the public blockchain, and exercising rights to deletion cannot apply to on-chain blockchain registries.
